Boost Me! – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 August 2025

Thank you for choosing Boost Me! (“the App”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Who we are

Boost Me! is developed and published by Manuel Radons (“we”, “us”, “our”). For any privacy-related questions, please contact boostmeappcontact@gmail.com.

2. Scope of this policy

This Policy applies to all versions of the Boost Me! Android application distributed through Google Play Store and any other official channels.

3. Data we collect

We do not collect: names, email addresses, location data, contacts, photos, microphone recordings or any other personally identifiable information (“PII”).

4. Third-party services

The App integrates the following third-party SDKs, each governed by its own privacy terms:

ServicePurposeLinks
Google AdMob & Google Mobile Ads SDK Display banner advertisements AdMob Privacy & Terms, Google Privacy Policy

Google may collect device information (e.g., Advertising ID, IP address) to deliver and measure ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in Android Settings ▸ Privacy ▸ Ads ▸ Delete Advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

5. Permissions the App requests

PermissionWhy it is needed
POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13 +) Show scheduled alarms and motivational reminders in your notification shade
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM Trigger alarms exactly at the time you set

The App functions if you deny these permissions, although alarms and notifications may be limited.

6. How we use your data

  • Provide core features such as displaying boosts, saving favourites, and scheduling alarms.
  • Display non-personalised or personalised ads (subject to your Android ad-personalisation setting).
  • Diagnose crashes and improve performance (aggregated statistics only).

We do not sell or rent any data. We do not transfer locally stored data off your device.

7. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

You have the right to withdraw consent at any time (see § 10).

8. Data retention

  • Locally stored data – kept on your device until you uninstall the App or use the in-app Reset App Data option.
  • AdMob / Analytics data – retained in accordance with Google’s policies.

9. Security

All sensitive operations (favourites, alarms) run entirely on-device. No remote server stores your personal data. Nevertheless, no mobile platform can guarantee absolute security; use the App at your own discretion.

10. Your rights (EEA/UK & similar jurisdictions)

Subject to local law, you may have the right to:

  1. Access the personal data we process about you;
  2. Rectify inaccuracies;
  3. Erase data in certain circumstances;
  4. Object to or restrict processing;
  5. Data portability for information you provided; and
  6. Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Because we hold no direct identifiers, fulfilling some requests may require you to share your device’s Advertising ID or other proof of use.

11. Children’s privacy

Boost Me! is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

12. International users

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. By using the App, you consent to such cross-border processing. Where required, we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced within the App or via the Google Play listing. Continued use of the App after changes implies acceptance.
