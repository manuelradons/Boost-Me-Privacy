Boost Me! – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 August 2025

Thank you for using Boost Me! (“the App”). Your privacy matters. This Policy explains what data the App processes, why, and the choices you have.

1. Who we are

Controller/Publisher: Manuel Radons (“we”, “us”, “our”). For privacy inquiries, email manuel.radons.dev@gmail.com.

2. Scope

This Policy applies to the Android app “Boost Me!” distributed via Google Play and our official channels.

5. Advertising & consent (EEA/UK/CH)

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we use a Consent Management Platform (Google UMP) under the IAB TCF 2.2 framework to collect your choices about ad-related storage and personalisation.

  • If you consent to personalised ads: Google may use identifiers to tailor ads to you.
  • If you do not consent to personalisation or ad storage: you will receive non-personalised ads (contextual) or, where required, no ads.
  • Change your mind anytime: In the App go to Settings ▸ Privacy & Consent to reopen the consent dialog.

You can also reset or delete your Android Advertising ID in Android Settings ▸ Privacy ▸ Ads.

6. Permissions we request

PermissionWhy it is needed
POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+) Show alarm reminders and motivational notifications.
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM Trigger alarms precisely at the time you set (device/OS may require a special allowance).
VIBRATE Haptic feedback for alarms and interactions.
INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Load and display ads; check connectivity.

The App works if you deny optional permissions, though alarms/notifications may be limited.

7. How we use data

  • Provide core features (boosts, favourites, exact alarms, settings).
  • Show ads (personalised only with your consent where required; otherwise non-personalised/contextual).
  • Improve stability and performance using aggregated store analytics.

We do not sell or share your personal information for monetary value. We do not transfer your local in-app data off your device.

8. Legal bases (GDPR)

Processing activityLegal basis

Non-personalised/contextual ads & fraud prevention Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) and/or consent where required by local law.

9. Retention

  • Local app data: remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear the App’s data.
  • Ad/measurement data: is retained by Google in accordance with its policies.

10. Security

Boost favourites, schedules and similar content are processed on-device. No remote server operated by us stores your in-app data. While we take care to use the platform securely, no mobile system can guarantee absolute security.

11. Your rights (EEA/UK & similar jurisdictions)

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, obtain data portability, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Because we do not maintain user accounts, we may ask for your device’s Advertising ID or other proof of use to locate relevant records.

12. International processing

Third-party providers (e.g., Google) may process data on servers outside your country. Where required, such transfers rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

13. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated in-app or on the store listing. Continued use of the App after changes implies acceptance.
