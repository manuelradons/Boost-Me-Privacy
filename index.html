Boost Me! – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 August 2025

1. Who we are

Boost Me! is developed and published by Manuel Radons ("we", "us", "our"). For any privacy-related questions, please contact boostmeappcontact@gmail.com.

2. Scope of this policy

This Policy applies to all versions of the Boost Me! Android application distributed through Google Play Store and any other official channels.

4. Third‑party services & how ad personalisation is controlled

The App integrates the following third‑party SDK, which is governed by its own privacy terms:

ServicePurposeLinks
Google AdMob & Google Mobile Ads SDK Display banner advertisements AdMob / Google Ads Policies · Google Privacy Policy

Google may collect device information (e.g., Advertising ID, IP address) to deliver and measure ads.

Important – Personalisation control: Whether ads are personalised or non‑personalised is determined exclusively by your Android system setting:

Android Settings › Google › Ads › Opt out of Ads Personalisation / Delete advertising ID.

The App does not present its own consent dialog and does not offer an in‑app toggle for ad personalisation. If your device is opted‑out (or an advertising ID is unavailable/deleted), the App requests non‑personalised ads; otherwise, Google may show personalised ads based on your device‑level choice.

5. Permissions the App requests (with reasons)

The App uses the following Android permissions. Where a permission is marked as normal, it is granted automatically and does not require a runtime prompt.

PermissionWhy it is neededNotes
INTERNET (normal) Load advertisements from AdMob Required for network access
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE (normal) Detect connectivity to avoid unnecessary requests and improve reliability
POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+) Show scheduled alarms and motivational reminders in your notification shade You can deny at runtime; the App still works but can’t notify
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM Trigger alarms exactly at the time you set Needed for precise alarms
USE_EXACT_ALARM (Android 14+) Required by newer Android versions when scheduling exact alarms Applies where available
VIBRATE (normal) Provide haptic feedback for alarms/notifications

The App functions if you deny runtime permissions, although alarms and notifications may be limited.

6. How we use your data

  • Provide core features such as displaying boosts, saving favourites, and scheduling alarms.
  • Display non‑personalised or personalised ads – exclusively controlled by your Android ad‑personalisation setting (see § 4). There is no in‑app consent flow.
  • Diagnose crashes and improve performance (aggregated statistics only).

We do not sell or rent any data. We do not transfer locally stored data off your device.

Processing activityLegal basis

8. Data retention

  • Locally stored data — kept on your device until you uninstall the App or use the in‑app Reset App Data option.
  • AdMob / Analytics data — retained in accordance with Google’s policies.

9. Security

All sensitive operations (favourites, alarms) run entirely on‑device. No remote server stores your personal data. Nevertheless, no mobile platform can guarantee absolute security; use the App at your own discretion.

10. Your rights (EEA/UK & similar jurisdictions)

Subject to local law, you may have the right to:

  1. Access the personal data we process about you;
  2. Rectify inaccuracies;
  3. Erase data in certain circumstances;
  4. Object to or restrict processing;
  5. Data portability for information you provided; and
  6. Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Because we hold no direct identifiers, fulfilling some requests may require you to share your device’s Advertising ID or other proof of use.

11. Children’s privacy

Boost Me! is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

12. International users

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. By using the App, you consent to such cross‑border processing. Where required, we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards.
